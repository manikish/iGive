I have something I want to give away. How do I post it?

Once you are logged in, tap on the plus at the lower-right corner of the home screen. It will take you to another screen where you can input your title, take a picture of your item, and a location to pick the item. After that, tap "Post It" then your item will show up on your others iGive.

What kind of items can I give?

Anything you think someone else out there might be better off with it. Be it lamp, desk, tennis racquet, etc. as long as it's useful and not prohibited.

What if I decide to keep my items after posting them?

You can delete your post by right-swiping it and choose "Delete". Your post will be removed from the system. If its already been requested, it is proper to inform the requester that you no longer wish to give it.

What do I get by giving away my item?

Mostly nothing. But if you think about it. You just did your mother nature a huge favor by not dumping your unused toys on her and you helped a stranger in need.

Can I upload more than one picture for an item?

No. Pick your best one.

How do I contact the owner of the item I want to request?

Once you see an item you need, tap on "Request Item", then input your name, email address and your number. iGive will send your contact details to the donor. Donor will contact you on further details.

Will my contact information be safe?

Your privacy is important to us and we only send it to the owners of the requested items.
